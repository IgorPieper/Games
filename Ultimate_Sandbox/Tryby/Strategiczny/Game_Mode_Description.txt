A key mode in Ultimate Sandbox is the Strategy Mode, inspired by the nostalgic fun of playing with toy soldiers. This mode provides a top-down, slightly angled view, where players command a large group of units tasked with specific objectives.

Each mission is unique and intriguing in its own way, categorized into two types: campaign missions and free missions.

Campaign missions provide players with a set of preselected units for use. Every completed mission unlocks a new unit for use, expanding players' strategic options.

Free missions allow players to choose any of the unlocked units and utilize them to accomplish tasks. Completing these missions unlocks new character skins, accessories, and other non-gameplay affecting elements, but greatly enhances the overall gaming appeal.
